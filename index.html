Who We Are

The Algorithm is a vibrant student-founded and student-led tech community at Padma Kanya Campus, dedicated to leveraging technology for the betterment of society and gender equality. We recognize that representation of females in STEM is very low, primarily due to a lack of instilled confidence, access to learning STEM concepts, role models, and more.

One common sentiment among the females in our university is that we are limiting ourselves due to a lack of confidence and imposter syndrome. Therefore, The Algorithm community focuses on helping females build confidence through hands-on experience and mentorship.

Senior year university students organize workshops and events for junior year students. As the junior students gain knowledge, the senior students, serving as organizers or mentors, also gain confidence by exposing themselves to leadership roles. In the past 10 months, our community has expanded beyond its initial scope. Initially, we had 8 executive members, but now we have added 3 more to our board. Additionally, we have positively impacted over 500 women through our events, workshops, and projects.

One of our significant milestones is being included in the Women in Data Steering Committee by The Asia Foundation, where we work on dismantling systemic barriers and fostering a more equitable future for women in the data and technology landscape. Currently, we are focusing on open data and how it can be leveraged to make data-driven policies. To this end, we are organizing hackathons outside the capital city, focusing on these themes. Together, we pave the way for a brighter, more inclusive tomorrow.

What We Address

Our community addresses the common challenges faced by women in technology and provides support to overcome these barriers:

  • Lack of Confidence and Imposter Syndrome Many of us struggle with self-doubt and imposter syndrome, which can limit our potential. We create safe spaces for learning and growth where members can practice skills and build confidence without judgment.
  • Leadership Skills Development We provide opportunities to practice and develop leadership skills through organizing events, workshops, and community initiatives. Every member gets a chance to lead and make meaningful contributions.
  • Professional Experience and Resume Building Gaining volunteering experience is important to us, as it helps in securing internships and building our resumes with real-world impact. Our initiatives provide tangible experience that employers value.
  • Mentoring and Supporting Newcomers We are passionate about supporting and mentoring newcomers in the tech field, creating a welcoming environment for all skill levels. Our peer-to-peer mentorship model ensures everyone feels supported.

Our Democratic Election Process

We believe in transparent and fair leadership transitions. Our election process ensures that every voice is heard and respected:

  • Announcement & Nominations The process begins with an official announcement inviting junior committee members to nominate themselves for leadership roles. Interested candidates must submit a nomination form that outlines their qualifications, relevant experience, and vision for their desired role. This ensures all candidates have a clear understanding of their potential responsibilities.
  • Campaigning Period Approved candidates are given a dedicated period to campaign within the community. This allows them to share their goals, proposed initiatives, and vision for their role. Campaigning may include "Meet the Candidates" sessions, presentations, and discussions to engage and inform the community, fostering transparency and trust.
  • Fair Voting Process The community conducts voting through a secret ballot process to maintain fairness and confidentiality. Voting is open to all eligible members (e.g., junior and senior committees) and can be facilitated in-person or through a secure online platform. This ensures everyone has an equal opportunity to voice their choice for the incoming leaders.
  • Results & Leadership Handover Once votes are counted and verified, the results are announced in a formal manner. A handover ceremony is held to celebrate the transition of leadership. Outgoing leaders provide mentorship and guidance to the incoming members, ensuring they are well-prepared for their new roles and responsibilities, fostering continuity and growth within the community.

Our Mission

Our community's mission is to build an ecosystem within the campus where everyone feels welcome to practice their leadership skills. The heart of our community is that senior year students organize workshops for junior year students. This approach allows senior students to lead events, boosting their confidence, while junior students benefit from the knowledge and experience of their seniors. An additional benefit for senior year students is that these leadership experiences enhance their resumes, making it easier to secure internships and career opportunities.

We envision a future where every woman in technology feels empowered, confident, and supported in her journey. Through our initiatives, we aim to create lasting change not just within our campus, but in the broader tech ecosystem of Nepal. Our goal is to become a model for other institutions and inspire similar communities across the region.
