Code of Conduct

Guidelines for creating a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment where every community member can thrive and contribute meaningfully.

Read Our Standards Report an Issue

Our Community Standards

The Algorithm is dedicated to empowering women in technology. We aim to create an inclusive, supportive, and respectful community where everyone feels welcome. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all members and participants in our community.

Core Values

  • Inclusivity & Diversity We welcome women of all backgrounds, identities, and experiences. By women we mean cisgender, transgender, and non-binary individuals who identify with the female experience. We celebrate diversity in all its forms.
  • Respect & Dignity Treat others with respect and kindness. Every person deserves to be treated with dignity regardless of their background, experience level, or perspective. Discrimination, harassment, or exclusionary behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Empowerment & Growth Support and uplift each other, fostering sisterhood and an environment where everyone can grow and succeed. We believe in collective growth and mutual empowerment.
  • Collaboration & Knowledge Sharing Encourage collaboration and knowledge sharing. Everyone has something valuable to contribute, regardless of their experience level. We learn together and grow together.
  • Integrity & Authenticity Act with honesty and integrity in all interactions. Be authentic, acknowledge mistakes, and commit to continuous learning and improvement.

Expected Behavior

We expect all community members to embody these behaviors in all community interactions:

  • Respectful Communication Engage in respectful, constructive, and inclusive discussions. Listen actively, communicate clearly, and be open to different perspectives and ideas.
  • Thoughtful Consideration Consider how your actions and words affect others. Refrain from making assumptions about others' backgrounds, abilities, or experiences. Practice empathy and understanding.
  • Supportive Environment Offer help and support to others, particularly those who are new or less experienced. Create a welcoming atmosphere where questions are encouraged and learning is celebrated.
  • Recognition & Attribution Recognize and value the contributions of others. Give credit where it's due and acknowledge the work and ideas of your fellow community members.
  • Professional Conduct Maintain professional standards in all community activities, both online and offline. Represent the community positively in external interactions.

Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will result in appropriate consequences:

  • Harassment & Intimidation Harassment of any kind, including but not limited to offensive comments, deliberate intimidation, stalking, unwelcome attention, or persistent unwanted contact.
  • Discrimination & Bias Discrimination based on gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion, nationality, or any other personal characteristic.
  • Disruptive Behavior Disruption of events, talks, workshops, or other community activities. This includes inappropriate use of online platforms and resources, spamming, or trolling.
  • Privacy Violations Sharing private information of others without their explicit consent, including personal details, private communications, or sensitive information.
  • Inappropriate Content Sharing or displaying sexually explicit, violent, or otherwise inappropriate content in community spaces, whether online or offline.

Consequences of Unacceptable Behavior

To maintain a safe and inclusive environment, we have established clear consequences for violations of this Code of Conduct:

  • Warning For minor violations, the person will receive a formal warning explaining why their behavior was inappropriate and what is expected of them in the future. This serves as an educational opportunity and a chance for course correction.
  • Temporary Suspension For more serious or repeated violations, the person may be temporarily suspended from community activities and events. The duration will depend on the severity of the violation.
  • Permanent Expulsion In cases of severe or continuous violations that threaten the safety and well-being of community members, the person may be permanently expelled from the community. This decision is made carefully and only when necessary.

Reporting and Enforcement

We are committed to addressing violations promptly and fairly. Here's how our reporting and enforcement process works:

  • How to Report If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the community leaders at thealgorithm0@gmail.com. All reports will be treated with confidentiality and taken seriously.
  • Investigation Process All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. We will gather all relevant information and may speak with witnesses or other involved parties. The investigation will be conducted with discretion and respect for all parties involved.
  • Taking Action Appropriate actions will be taken based on the severity and frequency of the behavior, following the consequences outlined above. We are committed to protecting the safety and well-being of all community members.
  • Support for Reporters We provide support for those who report violations and will not tolerate retaliation against anyone who reports concerns in good faith. Your courage in speaking up helps make our community better for everyone.

Our Commitment to Improvement

The Algorithm is committed to continuously improving our community standards and creating an ever more inclusive environment. We encourage feedback and suggestions from all members to help us create a safer and more welcoming space.

This Code of Conduct is a living document that evolves with our community. We regularly review and update these guidelines to ensure they remain relevant and effective in fostering the kind of community we all want to be part of.

Building Our Future Together

By participating in The Algorithm, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct. Together, let's build a community that truly empowers and supports women in tech, creating a brighter and more inclusive future for everyone.

Thank you for being part of our mission to create positive change in the technology landscape. Your commitment to these values helps us build a stronger, more supportive community for all.
